Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026 · MaxNova Tech (Sole trader, Kirkcaldy, Scotland)

This policy explains what personal data MaxNova Tech collects, why, and your rights under UK GDPR. MaxNova Tech is a sole trader business operated by Max Dorman.

1. What data I collect

When you use the contact form on this website, I collect:

  • Your name
  • Your email address
  • The message content you submit
  • The service type you select

I do not collect any other personal data through this website. I do not use cookies, analytics, or tracking scripts.

2. Why I collect it

The data you submit via the contact form is used solely to respond to your enquiry. The legal basis for processing this data is legitimate interest — you have contacted me to ask about a service, and I need your details to respond.

3. Who sees your data

Formspree — The contact form is processed by Formspree (formspree.io), a third-party service. Your submission is sent to their servers and forwarded to my email. Formspree's own privacy policy applies to this processing. I do not store form submissions anywhere else.

I do not sell, share, or pass your personal data to any other third parties.

4. How long I keep your data

Enquiries that don't lead to work are deleted within 6 months. If we work together, I'll retain contact details for as long as needed to provide the service and meet any legal obligations (e.g. tax records for 6 years).

5. Your rights under UK GDPR

You have the right to:

  • Request access to the personal data I hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Object to processing of your data

To exercise any of these rights, email me at maxd@ittechs.io and I'll respond within 30 days.

6. Complaints

If you're unhappy with how I've handled your data, you can contact the UK's data protection regulator: the Information Commissioner's Office (ICO) at ico.org.uk or 0303 123 1113.

7. Contact

For any privacy-related questions: maxd@ittechs.io
